A better way to remember.


Remember more about your vacation than what you can only see in your pictures. By recording your physiological state with the Smart Sole device while taking pictures and videos and uploading to this site, you can plot your entire trip's journey on a map. All your pictures will show up in the location they were taken, and information about your physiological state at that time will be displayed along side that picture, so you can know more about yourself and that moment.
